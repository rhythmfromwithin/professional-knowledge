# A Note on Diameter Certification in Trees
> 原文: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09929](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09929)

arXiv:2607.09929v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: In the local certification model, certifying the diameter of general graphs requires large certificates, but trees admit more efficient solutions. In this note, we provide a $1$-local certification scheme that certifies whether the diameter of a given tree is at most $d$ using certificates of at most $3\lceil\log\_2(d+1)\rceil$ bits.
