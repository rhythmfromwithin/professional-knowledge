# Now available: Amazon EC2 M9g and M9gd instances powered by new AWS Graviton5 processors
> 原文: [https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/now-available-amazon-ec2-m9g-and-m9gd-instances-powered-by-new-aws-graviton5-processors/](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/now-available-amazon-ec2-m9g-and-m9gd-instances-powered-by-new-aws-graviton5-processors/)

AWS launches Amazon EC2 M9g and M9gd instances, powered by AWS Graviton5 processors. AWS Graviton5 is most powerful, and most energy eﬃcient processor AWS has ever built, and oﬀers up to 25% better compute performance compared to Graviton4-based instances.
