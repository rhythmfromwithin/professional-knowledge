# Byzantine Consensus in Directed Graphs with Message Authentication
> 原文: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11309](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11309)

arXiv:2605.11309v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We consider the problem of reaching consensus in communication networks that are modeled by directed graphs. We assume the existence of a message authentication mechanism (such as digital signatures) to verify the integrity of messages. We identify the necessary and sufficient conditions on the directed communication graph for the following problems to be solvable: (i) exact consensus in synchronous systems; and (ii) approximate consensus in asynchronous systems.
